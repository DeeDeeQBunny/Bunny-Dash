This is my current ongoing project to make a website with the following details: 
Bunny Dash is a game where you have a character exploring a city of
bunnies called Bunnycity. To feed the bunnies in the city, you need to
collect carrots in carrot treasure chests or farming. There are also
quests to complete to get bunny coins, the currency of Bunnycity. You
can use these bunny coins to buy items for your character or get more
carrots. When the bunnies are fed, you can explore the structures in
the city as long as you want. New structures, quests, and bunnies will
be added in future updates. Have fun!

The controls are simple. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move. To look
in a different direction, use a mouse, trackpad, or slide the screen.
To interact with objects or bunnies press the S key or the interact
button once a popup appears. To open the inventory, press the I key or
the open inventory button. To see your quests, press the Q key or the
quests button. To see the city map, press the M key or the map button.
To see your stats, press the P key or the stats button. In the stats
page there is also the store of bunny coins, where you can buy items
for your character or get more carrots. The settings can also be found
there, where you can change the volume, graphics, and other settings.
The credits are also in this page. To see the controls, press the C
key or the controls button.
